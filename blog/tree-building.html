Building a Family Tree with DNA and Records

How to combine traditional genealogy with genetic evidence to create a verified, multi-generational tree.

Home Blog

Step 1: Start with What You Know

Gather birth, marriage, and death records. Interview relatives. Build a basic tree.

Step 2: Layer in DNA Matches

Use shared matches and segment data to verify relationships and uncover unknown branches.

Step 3: Document Everything

Source citations, match notes, and visual charts help preserve your research and share it with others.
