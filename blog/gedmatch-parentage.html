How to Use GEDmatch for Unknown Parentage Cases

A step-by-step guide to using One-to-Many, Segment Search, and triangulation to identify biological relatives.

Home Blog

Step 1: Upload Your Raw DNA

Start by uploading your raw DNA file to GEDmatch. Choose a public kit if you're comfortable sharing matches.

Step 2: Use One-to-Many Tool

This tool shows your top matches. Focus on those with high cM and shared segments.

Step 3: Segment Search & Triangulation

Use Tier 1 tools to find overlapping segments among matches. Build a hypothesis tree based on shared ancestors.
