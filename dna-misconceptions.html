DNA Doesn’t Lie—But It Can Confuse You

Common misconceptions about ethnicity estimates, match relationships, and unexpected results.

Home Blog

Myth 1: Ethnicity = Identity

Ethnicity estimates are just that—estimates. They’re based on reference populations and can vary by platform.

Myth 2: Match Labels Are Always Accurate

A “second cousin” might actually be a half-uncle. Relationship predictions depend on shared DNA, not family structure.

Myth 3: DNA Is Always Clear

Endogamy, pedigree collapse, and low cM matches can muddy the waters. That’s where analysis comes in.
